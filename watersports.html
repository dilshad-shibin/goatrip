About the Grand Island Goa Water Sports:

A holiday in Goa is simply incomplete without experiencing a Grand Island trip. The island popularly known as Bat island or Monkey island is a place that will fill you with excitement and thrill. Gear up for one of the best scuba experiences at Grand Island Goa with its clear water and beautiful marine life. Enjoy the breathtaking underwater views and the amazing marine environment while diving a maximum of 30 feet under the sea. Indulge in a range of water sports like the Banana ride, Speed Boat ride, Bumper ride, and Jetski, and spend a fun-filled day with your friends here.

  • Pick-up for the enthralling activities will be at 7 AM and pick-up locations would be Calangute, Baga, Candolim, Arpora.
  • The trip will be starting from Sinquerim Boat Jetty in a Long Tail boat at 8:30 AM to the Grand Island where you can witness amazing views to soothe your senses.
  • Enjoy 45-60 minutes of a boat ride to the vicinity of Grand islands till 10:30 AM. If you are lucky enough, you might have a chance to spot dolphins during the journey.
  • Curb your hunger pangs at 10:30 AM with sumptuous meals such as light snacks and lunch.
  • Get refreshing and chilled soft drinks or beer (3 pints per person) as complimentary.
  • Relish the astonishing underwater views for about 15 minutes depending upon the factors like weather and pressure of water.
  • Grab the most thrilling water sports experience at 4:00 PM that includes a Banana ride, Speed Boat ride, Bumper ride, and Jetski. (Included in certain packages).
  • Activities will be performed with proper safety and with all the necessary safety equipment.
  • Get yourself dropped on the drop-off locations same as mentioned above at 5:30-6:00 PM.
